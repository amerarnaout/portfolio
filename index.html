About

I don't just make things look good — I build visual systems that make brands perform better.

I started as a Computer Engineer — so I think in systems, not aesthetics. Every visual decision connects to a business outcome: higher engagement, better conversion, stronger retention.

Over 4+ years across Dubai, New York, and Damascus, I've built brand identities from scratch, designed software interfaces, and run social strategy for multiple brands simultaneously. Engineering logic applied to visual craft.

Currently open to senior designer roles, brand projects, and long-term creative partnerships.
